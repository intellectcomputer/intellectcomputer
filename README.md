Intellect Computers is a government-authorized IT training institute in Surat with a 25+ year history of career-focused education【23†L113-L118】.  Founded in 2000 by Mrs. Radhika Saraiya【20†L344-L347】【25†L115-L118】, it operates as a “career launchpad” – not just a coaching center – combining university-subject coaching (BCA/MCA) with industry-oriented internships, live project training and placement support【5†L37-L39】【23†L116-L118】. ## Legacy & History  
Intellect Computers was established in Surat in 2000 by Mrs. Radhika Saraiya【20†L344-L347】.  Over 25+ years, the institute has built a reputation for trusted **education excellence**【23†L113-L118】. Intellect’s history is marked by steady growth (surpassing 1,000+ alumni) and a decade-spanning record of students achieving top ranks in their universities【20†L344-L347】【20†L349-L352】. 

## Mission & Values  
Intellect Computers’ mission is to provide **practical, career-oriented IT education**.  “Teaching is not just my profession; it is my lifelong passion,” says founder Radhika Saraiya【5†L48-L52】, and the institute’s programs reflect this philosophy.  The *Intellect Way* emphasizes real-world skills: students **code from day one**, working on live applications and internships rather than just theory【5†L58-L60】【23†L101-L104】.  Core values include: 

- **Practical Coding:** Hands-on software development (not just lectures) to build real-world skills【5†L72-L75】.  
- **Career Growth:** Honest mentorship and personal guidance (small batches of 8–15) to prepare capable professionals【5†L77-L80】【23†L101-L104】.  
- **Live Projects:** Industry-aligned internships and final-year projects for clear insight into how technology works in practice【5†L58-L60】【5†L82-L84】.  

Together, these values have guided Intellect’s approach: students learn with *full confidence*, often achieving high exam results and securing IT jobs quickly after graduation【5†L58-L60】【23†L116-L118】.
